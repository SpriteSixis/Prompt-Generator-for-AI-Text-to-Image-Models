Full chaos

Create multiple template cards and mark the ones you want in rotation as Active. Each generated prompt will roulette-pick one active card; if none are active, we fall back to simple random words. Lock makes a card read-only, Duplicate/Del let you iterate fast.

  • Reminder: Ensure the [CATEGORY] names in the template match the ones in the categories section (e.g. [SUBJECTS] instead of [SUBJECT]).
  • Don't forget to check your 'Include' sliders, if they are deactivated, the [CATEGORY] won't appear in the prompt, even if it is written in the Template.
  • Finally, don't forget to check that the Number of Words in your [CATEGORY] container is at least 1.
  • If no template cards are active, generation will fall back to randomized words from your selected categories without any specific sentence structure. The words will be generated in the order of the categories from top to bottom.
